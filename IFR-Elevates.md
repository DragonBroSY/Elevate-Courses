- MARVELOSVFRC500
	- M - Missed approach execution (request clearance for specific action).
	- A - Airspeed changes of ±10 knots or 5% of filed True Airspeed (whichever is greater).  
	    
	- R - Reaching a holding fix (report time and altitude).  
	    
	- V - VFR-on-Top altitude changes.  
	    
	- E - ETA changes of more than ±3 minutes (required only in non-radar environments).  
	    
	- L - Leaving a holding fix or point.  
	    
	- O - Outer marker inbound (required only in non-radar environments).  
	    
	- U - Unforecasted weather conditions encountered.  
	    
	- S - Safety of flight issues (e.g., equipment malfunction).  
	    
	- V - Vacating an assigned altitude.  
	    
	- F - Final approach fix inbound (required only in non-radar environments).  
	    
	- R - Radio or navigation equipment failure.  
	    
	- C - Compulsory reporting points (required only in non-radar environments).
